Buhrman's Store

This general store opened in 1828 establishing it as the first trading post in Northeastern Queens.

The store was situated in the area known as "The Alley": the present site of the Long Island Expressway and Cross Island Parkway interchange.

The original properietor, Benjamin Lowerre, a former inkeeper from Flushing, ran the store for eleven years. He subsequently sold it to his son-in-law, William C. Buhrman, on April 30, 1839.
